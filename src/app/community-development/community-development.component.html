Participatory Community Development
The Participatory Community Development project of Nirmaan works with the residents of bastis in and around Pilani to improve their quality of life and equip them with basic day-today necessities. This seeks to engage local populations in development projects in order to make the projects more sustainable and successful.
Udaan
Project Udaan is aimed at youth empowerment by finding possible employment opportunities and providing upskilling courses to beneficiaries, thus enabling them to pursue careers in various fields of their choice. The team successfully organized guidance sessions for beneficiaries, which included career counseling, motivational talks, and personalized guidance to help them make informed decisions about their future. Volunteers reached out to beneficiaries to understand their employment needs and created a database to support initiatives like BTTI.
